Donation Central

Amidst the COVID-19 crisis, many families and individuals are in need of items such as masks, food, clothing, and more. At the same time, many people are finding time to go through their belongings and finding items to donate. However, as a result of social distancing regulations, many donation sites are closed. This website is a resource for people in the Bay Area to donate their belongings in order to support those affected by COVID-19.
